RNDR

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

RNDR is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use RNDR.

2. Information We Collect

RNDR collects and processes the following information:

  • TikTok API Credentials: Your TikTok access tokens and refresh tokens (stored locally on your computer)
  • Local Files: Video assets, audio files, captions, and lyrics stored on your computer (never transmitted to external servers)
  • Configuration Data: Workflow settings, schedules, and preferences (stored locally on your computer)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf
  • Create and post video content to your TikTok account
  • Fetch analytics data from TikTok
  • Render videos using your local assets
  • Store your workflow configurations

4. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your computer. We do not transmit, store, or process your personal data on external servers or in the cloud (unless you choose cloud deployment as an optional feature).

5. Data Security

You are responsible for securing your computer and keeping your TikTok credentials safe. RNDR stores credentials locally using standard encryption practices. Keep your computer password-protected and do not share API keys.

6. Third-Party Services

RNDR integrates with TikTok's API. Any data sent to TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing TikTok's privacy practices.

7. Data We Do NOT Collect

RNDR does not:

  • Collect analytics or usage data about how you use the application
  • Store your videos or content on external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell or monetize your personal information
  • Track your location or browsing behavior

8. Cookies and Tracking

RNDR is a desktop application and does not use cookies or tracking technology.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all RNDR data by removing the application folder from your computer. This will erase all stored credentials, configurations, and local data.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how RNDR handles your data, please contact the application creator.

12. Compliance

RNDR is designed to be compliant with privacy best practices. Since it operates locally on your machine, it does not require GDPR, CCPA, or other data privacy law compliance in the same way as cloud-based services.
