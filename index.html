Automate Your TikTok

Create, customize, and post videos to TikTok automatically. Smart video automation for creators.

What is RNDR?

RNDR is a desktop application that automates video creation and posting to TikTok. Generate multiple videos daily using your assets, music, and captions. Test different approaches, track performance, and optimize your content strategy—all while you focus on creating great music.

Key Features

⚙️

Automated Posting

Set schedules and let RNDR post your videos automatically every day.

📊

Performance Tracking

Monitor analytics and see which videos perform best with real data.

🎵

Multi-Song Support

Test different song sections and track which ones resonate with viewers.

✨

Flexible Customization

Lyric videos, text overlays, custom captions, and hashtags—full control.

🔄

Smart Workflows

Create multiple independent workflows with different assets and settings.

💾

Local & Private

All processing happens on your computer. Your data stays yours.
